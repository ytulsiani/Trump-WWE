Why We Made This

On July 2nd 2017, United States President Donald Trump tweeted an edited video clip of him bodyslamming CNN. Since then many have called this an attack on our free press and a coveted threat to our civil liberties. CNN made a statement saying, "It is a sad day when the President of the United States encourages violence against reporters" and we agree. Implied violence and demonization of the press are not jokes; especially when initiated by the leader of the free world.
